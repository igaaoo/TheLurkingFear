# TheLurkingFear
## Avoid obstacles while killing ghosts to survive!
## Stay alive for 300 seconds to win, lose your sanity to die!

Commands:
Mouse - Look Around
A / D - Move
Left Click - Turn ON/OFF UV Flashlight
Right Click - Zoom In
